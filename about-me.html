About Me

I'm a Futures Brokerage Analyst and Citizen Developer at Marex, where my journey began with a simple need: automating my own repetitive tasks. But when the head of clearing needed interest calculations and IT estimated over a year for delivery, I delivered a working Python script in hours. That moment crystallized the power of citizen development - providing agile solutions when traditional processes move too slowly.

What started as solving my own operational problems evolved into something bigger when colleagues across departments began reaching out after seeing the results. This organic growth demonstrates how citizen development thrives in large enterprises: start by solving your own problems, demonstrate value, and watch as departments reach out for help. I've learned that having agile problem-solving options alongside formal IT processes creates the speed advantage that modern businesses desperately need.

My passion lies in bridging the gap between immediate business needs and enterprise-scale solutions. I work directly with data teams, collaborate with IT governance, and ensure solutions meet enterprise standards while maintaining the agility that business users require. Every manual process I encounter represents an opportunity to give someone their time back and free them to focus on work that truly matters.

Ready to see how citizen development transforms enterprise efficiency? Let's connect and explore what's possible.

My Approach to Problem-Solving

There's something incredibly satisfying about sitting down with a team, understanding their real problems, and knowing that together we can figure out a solution. I believe the most important thing isn't having all the technical answers - it's understanding the business problem deeply enough that the right solution reveals itself.

My approach centers on speed and agility without sacrificing enterprise standards. I don't replace formal IT processes; I complement them. By working directly with data teams and collaborating with IT governance, I ensure solutions meet enterprise requirements while delivering the rapid results that business users need.

The technology landscape evolves rapidly, especially in AI and automation. I'm constantly researching next-generation technologies, building proof-of-concept systems, and exploring how emerging tools can solve tomorrow's enterprise challenges. Currently, I'm diving deep into multi-agent AI systems, exploring how technologies like CrewAI and Langchain can create more sophisticated automation solutions for financial services.
