Challenge & Solution

As a futures brokerage analyst, my initial automation work gained attention across departments. The head of clearing reached out when Marex had no way to calculate interest on options for EOD and ITD reporting. With client money at stake and IT estimating over a year for delivery, I developed a Python script solution in hours, demonstrating citizen development's speed advantage.

The solution evolved through three phases: immediate Python fix → enhanced validation and business rules → enterprise Alteryx integration with full compliance and audit capabilities.

Technical Implementation

  • Multiple calculation formulas based on instrument type with automated parameter selection
  • Comprehensive validation against historical data and market benchmarks
  • Automated reporting with stakeholder distribution and full audit trails
  • Enterprise integration through Alteryx for compliance requirements

Results & Impact

  • Speed: Delivered in hours vs. IT's 1+ year timeline
  • Efficiency: Reduced daily processing from 4 hours to 15 minutes
  • Accuracy: Eliminated manual calculation errors (100% accuracy)
  • Compliance: Met regulatory requirements with full audit trails
  • Scalability: Handles 10x more calculations without additional resources

Enterprise Value Proposition

This project demonstrates how citizen development provides crucial options for rapid problem-solving in large enterprises. By delivering solutions in hours vs. years, it showcases the speed advantage over traditional IT while maintaining enterprise standards through collaboration with IT governance rather than replacement.

The solution evolved from immediate Python fix to enterprise-grade Alteryx integration, proving that citizen development can start small and scale to meet compliance and audit requirements. This approach creates options that bypass traditional red tape restrictions while ensuring regulatory compliance and operational excellence.
