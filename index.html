November 2023 - Present

Interest Rate Calculation Engine

The Challenge: The head of clearing reached out after seeing my initial automation work. Marex had no way to calculate interest on options for EOD and ITD reporting. Client money was at stake, compliance deadlines were approaching, and IT estimated over a year to deliver a solution.

The Speed Advantage: I developed a Python script that solved the problem in hours - demonstrating the speed advantage of citizen development over traditional IT processes. This evolved through phases: immediate Python fix → enhanced validation → enterprise Alteryx integration → full automation with audit trails.

The Enterprise Value: Delivered in hours vs. IT's 1+ year timeline, reduced daily processing from 4 hours to 15 minutes, eliminated manual errors, and met regulatory requirements with full compliance. This showcased how citizen development bypasses red tape while maintaining enterprise standards.

"This project demonstrated that in large enterprises, having options to solve problems quickly is crucial - especially when traditional IT red tape restricts solution delivery speed."

October 2023 - Present

Data Processing and Democratization Pipeline

The Data Democratization Challenge: Multiple departments were manually extracting the same data, creating bottlenecks and inconsistencies. Raw data from various sources needed transformation into digestible, structured formats for Finance and other teams.

The Collaborative Solution: Working directly with our data team, I implemented Alteryx-based data processing workflows that automated transformation of raw data into analysis-ready formats. This created standardized data pipelines feeding multiple downstream users.

The Enterprise Impact: Finance and other teams now have direct access to structured data through automated scheduling and quality assurance frameworks. This enhanced partnership between business users and the data team while ensuring data integrity and consistency.

"This project showed how citizen development can enhance collaboration with IT and data teams rather than replace them - creating enterprise-grade solutions with business user agility."
